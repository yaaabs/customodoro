üçÖ What is the Pomodoro Technique?

"The secret to effective time management is thinking in tomatoes rather than hours. It may seem silly initially, but millions of people swear by the life-changing power of the Pomodoro Technique. (Pomodoro is Italian for tomato. üçÖ)"

This revolutionary time management method alternates focused work sessions (called "pomodoros") with frequent short breaks. By breaking your workday into strategic 25-minute focus blocks, you can maintain high concentration and prevent mental fatigue.

The technique is beautifully simple yet powerful: work for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After completing four pomodoros, reward yourself with a longer 15-30 minute break. This rhythm helps your brain stay fresh and motivated throughout the day.
