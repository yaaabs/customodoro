Locked In Mode

What is it? A distraction-free, dark environment that activates automatically when your timer starts.

Benefits:

  • Minimalist interface shows only essential timer info
  • Hides distracting elements for maximum focus
  • Creates a calm, productive workspace

💡 Pro Tip: Use Locked In Mode with fullscreen (F11 or fullscreen button) for the ultimate distraction-free experience that covers your entire screen!

Auto-start Pomodoros

What are Auto-start Pomodoros?

When enabled, this feature automatically starts your next work session (pomodoro) as soon as a break ends.

Benefits of Auto-start Pomodoros:

  • Maintains your productivity momentum
  • Reduces the chance of extending breaks too long
  • Helps you get back to work immediately after breaks

How settings work together:

  • Auto-start Pomodoros ON: Timer will automatically transition from break to work
  • Auto-start Pomodoros OFF: Timer stops after break, requiring manual start for next work session
